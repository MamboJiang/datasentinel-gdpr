## Fast visual checklist

| Step | Where to look | Done when |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Confirm scope | Top-left Workspace control and page title | You know which Workspace owns the visible data. |
| Check scan state | Dashboard metric cards and latest scan panel | Scan progress, warnings, duration, and throughput are visible. |
| Open review work | Review focus or Findings | High-risk, owner decisions, retention reviews, and owner routing are visible. |
| Submit a decision | Finding detail review support | Allowed actions, denied actions, reason fields, and checklist items are visible before submission. |
| Prove the result | Audit Trail and Evaluation | A review event and quality/resource metrics are visible without legal-compliance claims. |

## What counts as complete?

- A source was selected or registered.
- A full scan started against an explicit source.
- Findings were visible only with redacted evidence.
- A human decision was recorded with a reason.
- The audit trail showed the workflow event.
- Evaluation metrics stayed visible and did not imply legal compliance.
